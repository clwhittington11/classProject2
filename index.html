Brooklyn Heights Pilates

Brooklyn Heights Pilates invites everyone to reap the benefits of the work created by Joseph Pilates in a peaceful and inviting studio where you will learn all the mechanics of pilates.

Brooklyn Heights Pilates offers Pilates reformer classes, Pilates mat classes, as well as private and duet instruction.

The Pilates workouts will dramatically change your body awareness, posture, alignment, flexibility, strength, and muscle tone. Our instructors are classically certified, highly educated and each brings his or her own unique perspective to the method.
